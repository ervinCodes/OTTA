Kathy Riley

Kathy Riley is the Founder and CEO of One Team Talent Agency ( OTTA ) with over three decades of experience in Production Accounting, ranging from Entry Level to Department Head, including a stint as a Director of Production Finance for a major television studio. Kathy has experienced first-hand the challenges faced when staffing Production Accounting departments. In response to these challenges, Kathy founded One Team Talent Agency ( OTTA ) with Jim Nicholson. Together they created a system designed to automate and update the process of staffing Production Accounting Teams in film and television. Additionally, OTTA offers a range of services to serve and support the production accounting community.

Throughout her career as a production accountant, Kathy specialized in 1-hr scripted, single-camera television dramas, and has worked with top studios such as ABC Signature Studios, Disney Channel, NBC-Universal, Warner Brothers Television, Sony Pictures, and 20th Century Fox. Kathy's proven leadership and extensive experience in the industry, combined with her strong commitment to improving the staffing process, is the driving force behind establishing One Team Talent Agency, which aims to offer innovative solutions to the production accounting community.

Jim Nicholson is the Co-Founder and Chief Information Officer of One Team Talent Agency. With over 30 years of experience in the IT field, both in private and corporate settings, Jim brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the company. In his current role as the IT Director for a major corporation based in Los Angeles, Jim is responsible for implementing various technology projects designed to enhance the efficiency and cost-effectiveness of the company's sales, service, HR, and business office functions. Prior to that, he served as CTO at Alliance United Insurance Services, where he built the IT infrastructure for the growing company. Throughout his career, Jim has been in charge of a wide range of IT functions, including enterprise data, user support, infrastructure, telecommunications, and information security. Jim's leadership and experience in the field of IT will be instrumental in driving One Team Talent Agency's technology strategy forward.

Meagen Brooks

Meagen Brooks is a Graphic Designer in ADG Local 800, having worked for television shows such as Weeds, Young Sheldon, Atypical and Saved By The Bell. After having completed her BS in Cinema and Photography at Ithaca College she moved to Los Angeles to pursue her artistic endeavors in both the art and film worlds. She is well versed with Adobe Creative Suite - Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and has working knowledge in Vectorworks and Sketchup Pro. She is also proficient in neon sign design and fabrication. She has been nominated for two Art Director's Guild Awards for graphic design in television and owns and operates her own graphic design company, Flying Dinosaur Designs, LLC, which services multiple industries.
