Privacy Policy

One Team Talent Agency Inc. aka OTTA, Privacy Policy

One Team Talent Agency Inc. is committed to protecting your personal information collected on our website. This Privacy Policy document describes the personal information we collect, how that information is used, and the steps we take to protect it.

We only provide services to OTTA Members and Studio/Production Members. All members are required to provide personal information when registering for membership with OTTA. This Privacy Policy will apply to all members of OTTA.

One Team Talent Agency does not store your credit card or banking information on our database. This information and financial transaction are processed by a third party (STRIPE)


Privacy Policy Changes

When we need to make changes to this Privacy Policy we will indicate the change by incrementing the version number located at the bottom of the Privacy Policy document.

How we use OTTA Members' personal information

We will use the personal information provided by OTTA members to:

Provide you with our service which includes pairing your information with Studio/Productions for employment consideration based on best fit and availability dates.

Contact you via email or phone when necessary


How we use Studio/Production Members' personal information

We will use personal information provided by Studio/Production Members to:

Provide job request results.

Send you information and links to the Star Review system

Give you updates to your requests.

Contact you via email or phone when necessary

Only OTTA Members and Studio/Production Members will receive your personal information from OTTA and only when it relates to employment situations.

We may use your personal information to develop analytics about site usage, traffic patterns, the success of system functionality, etc. This will be aggregated data that does not include or expose your personal information.
