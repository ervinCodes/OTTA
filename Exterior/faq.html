What is OTTA?


OTTA (One Team Talent Agency) is an automated platform that connects production accounting professionals with film and television productions in need of their expertise. Our system streamlines the job matching process—saving you time and enhancing your career opportunities.


Why should I join?


Joining OTTA gives you an additional, proactive way to access job opportunities. You can also continue using your current methods for job-searching while receiving real-time notifications for roles that match your preferred criteria.


What does it cost?


For just $1.00, you gain lifetime membership—whether you're a Department Head or a Clerk. Employers pay a fee when they use the platform. OTTA will never charge members commissions for jobs.


What if I don't need help finding work and have a network that has kept me steadily employed?


Sticking with a sure thing makes sense. However, what if an opportunity came along that was too good to pass up? Join OTTA, fill out your profile. The system is capable of matching you with jobs that meet or exceed your threshold. It's not about looking for work; it's about keeping an iron in the fire for the right opportunities. If someone is offering a rate so compelling that it makes sense to entertain a new production company, you will know about it. If not, nothing changes. No obligations, no wasted time-just the ability to stay informed about premium opportunities.


I'm a Department Head—what if I want to keep my team together but need additional help?


OTTA supports team continuity. Feel free to contact OTTA Admin at (818) 851-6882 or kathyriley@ottainc.com with any questions. We are here to help!


How does OTTA work?


  • Registration: Click “Join Now” on our website and follow the step-by-step process with our Helper Text, Quick Tips, and User's Manual/Video guidance.
  • Profile Setup: Complete your Resume of Qualifications (ROQ) and submit your profile for review.
  • Approval & Availability: After OTTA Admin approves your profile, you'll receive a “Welcome to the Team” email with instructions to update your availability—crucial for matching your resume to job requests.
  • Matching & Updates: Our system automatically sends your resume to employers up to six weeks before your designated stop date, tracks hires, and updates your resume and availability dates on all OTTA shows
***Learn even more by attending “Intro to OTTA” webinars on Zoom. Find out about webinars and catch all updates - join our private Facebook group, “One Team Talent Agency”.

Why do you use “Stop Date” instead of “Wrap Date”?


The “Stop Date” marks the end of your unavailability or - in other words - the date you stop being unavailable. It factors in your wrap date plus any additional personal time (e.g., vacation). For example, if your wrap date is 10/3/25 and you need two extra weeks off, your stop date becomes 10/19/25—making you available on 10/20/25. Always update your stop date if your schedule changes.


What is a Pinch Hitter and how does it work?


The Pinch Hitter program offers qualified short-term assistance for employers in film & television production. By checking the 'Pinch Hitter' option in your ROQ - Related Skills area, your resume will be submitted in response to employer's job requests from Department Heads to Clerks.


How do we find jobs?


OTTA automatically forwards your resume to employers whose job requests match your skills and availability. You can monitor these submissions in real time from your dashboard, which displays the date, company, show name, role, and wage range.


Where will I see job listings?


OTTA doesn't feature traditional job listings. Instead, every time your resume is sent, it's recorded in your Resume Activity feed on your dashboard—eliminating the need to sift through unrelated postings.


How will employers find me?


When matched with a job, employers receive your OTTA 5-show resume along with your contact information. If they need more details, they can request your full resume via email.


How will I know which jobs I'm being considered for?


Your Member Dashboard's Resume Activity feed keeps you informed by showing every job request your resume has been sent to in real time. When your resume is sent to an employer, the information simultaneously appears in your dashboard.


What if I work on productions not connected to OTTA?


For non-OTTA productions, you'll need to manually update your “Resume of Qualifications” and Availability Dates. Only verified OTTA-related credits are automatically updated and marked with an asterisk.


When using OTTA, aren't we paying for jobs?


No. The $1 Lifetime Membership Fee is necessary for internet security and provides a second form of identity verification. OTTA will never charge commissions for jobs.


Is my data secure?


Yes. OTTA uses industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and professional information. Your resume is only shared with employers in response to specific job requests.


How quickly will I be matched with job opportunities?


Matching depends on factors such as your availability, skill set, and current employer demand. Our system continually works to connect you with opportunities. Ultimately, as it has always been, it's the employer's decision.


Can I decline a job offer?


Absolutely. You retain full control over which opportunities you accept—review and decline job offers based on your preferences and schedule.


Will I receive feedback from employers?


While OTTA facilitates the connection, feedback is provided at the discretion of the employers. We encourage you to seek and value constructive feedback from employers to aid your professional growth.


What kind of support does OTTA provide?


Our customer support team is available via email and phone to help with any questions or issues you might encounter. We're here to ensure your experience with OTTA is smooth and successful.


What if I need to take a break from job searching?


You can easily update your availability status to reflect your break. For longer or more complex hiatuses, please contact OTTA Admin for personalized assistance.


How will I be paid once I accept a job?


Payments are handled directly between you and the employer.


Can I refer other production accounting professionals to OTTA?


Yes, please! The more OTTA members we have enrolled nationwide, the more employers we can serve. In time, we envision employers counting on OTTA for all of their shows. Share OTTA with colleagues and help us expand opportunities sooner across the industry.


Is OTTA available internationally?


Currently, OTTA operates in the USA only. We're exploring future opportunities for international expansion.
