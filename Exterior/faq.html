What is it?


OTTA is the game-changing system designed by One Team Talent Agency - it connects production accounting professionals with productions in need of their expertise


How does it work?


Once production accounting professionals decide to join OTTA, they select “Join Now” on our website, which takes them to the Registration page. We highly recommend accessing the User's Manual and/or User Video to guide you through entering your employment history, skills, preferences, and work status and/or availability dates. After completing the registration, account page, and employment history, the most important task for members is to update their availability in the “Current Status” area of the Team Member Dashboard. OTTA's system automatically matches qualified and available members to employers' job requests. It forwards your resume in response to an employer's job request SIX WEEKS in advance of your “stop date.” The system also tracks hires, auto-updates resumes, and accumulates verified, reliable, and measurable data.


Why do you call it “Stop Date” instead of “Wrap Date”?


The “Stop Date” reflects the date you become available for work again - whether it's because a show ends or you have extended your availability date to take a vacation or for any other personal reason you may have for being unavailable for work.


What does it cost?


For members, it's $1 for a lifetime membership. Companies using the service will be charged a fee based on the services provided.


What's a Pinch Hitter?


A Pinch Hitter provides short-term help on a production, whether to assist the team in catching up, fill in for someone, or help with insurance claims, among other tasks. Members can choose when and if they'd like to be a Pinch Hitter, and employers can specify when they need this type of short-term assistance.


How do we find jobs?


The OTTA system does the job search for you. It shows where your resume has been sent, including the date, company, show name, role, and wage range. Your task is to manage your availability dates in the system and nail your interviews.


Where will we see job listings?


You will see listings that match the criteria you've provided in your Resume Activity on your Member Dashboard. No need to waste time on jobs you're not interested in.


How will employers find me?


When matched with a job request, the employer will receive your resume along with your contact information.


How will I know what jobs I'm up for?


You will see your Resume Activity on your Member Dashboard.


What if I work on a non-OTTA-related show?


You will need to manually update your "Resume of Qualifications" in the "Add Credit" section. Only OTTA-related shows are automatically updated and marked with an asterisk before the date to indicate they are verified OTTA credits.


When we use OTTA, aren't we paying for jobs?


No, you are paying to be in the system and to be matched with employers looking for your skills. OTTA provides administrative services to save you time and effort, such as resume services, availability tracking, wage transparency, deal tracking, and resources.


Is my data secure?


Yes, OTTA takes data security seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to ensure your personal and professional information is safe


How quickly will I be matched with job opportunities?


Matching times can vary based on your availability, skills, and the current demand from employers. However, OTTA's automated system ensures that you are matched as soon as a suitable opportunity arises.


Can I decline a job offer?


Yes, you have the flexibility to accept or decline job offers based on your preferences and availability.


Will I receive feedback from employers?


While OTTA facilitates the connection between you and employers, we encourage employers to provide feedback.


What kind of support does OTTA provide?


OTTA offers customer support to assist you with any issues or questions you may have. Support is available via email and phone.


How do I become a Pinch Hitter?


To become a Pinch Hitter, update your preferences in the Related Skills area of your Resume of Qualifications on the OTTA website to indicate your interest in short-term assignments.


What happens if I need to take a break from job searching?


You can update your availability status to indicate the date you anticipate returning. If it's a significant break contact OTTA Admin for assistance.


How do payments work?


Payments for your services will be handled directly between you and the employer. OTTA does not take a commission from your earnings.


Can I refer other professionals to OTTA?


Yes, we encourage and appreciate. We are stronger together.


Is OTTA available internationally?


Currently, OTTA operates in USA only.
