OUR VISION

At One Team Talent Agency, we set the standard for Production Accounting staffing solutions. Our comprehensive system, cutting-edge technology, and unparalleled service differentiate us from our competitors. Our unwavering commitment to integrity, innovation, ethics, and teamwork drives us to continuously improve and innovate. We prioritize diversity, inclusion, equity, and belonging. We believe in cultivating a culture of excellence and empowering our employees and members to make a positive impact in the entertainment industry and the communities we serve.

MISSION

To connect production accounting professionals with productions in need of their expertise and support the production accounting community with their cutting-edge platform that tracks availability, performance and celebrates success.

VALUES

One Team Talent Agency believes in the diversity of people, integrity in our work, collaboration of skills, sharing knowledge and appreciating success.
