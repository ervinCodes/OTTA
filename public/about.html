Jim Nicholson

Jim Nicholson has been in the Information Technology field for over 30 years. His interest in computers started in the early eighties and his first computer was a Tandy TRS 80. In the early nineties Jim used his computer knowledge to create detailed construction estimates that won him most of the jobs he bid on. While working in the construction field, Jim took computer science classes at CSUN. He left the construction business in the mid eighties and went to work for a startup property and casualty incusrance company called Allince United.He became CTO at Alliance United, later called Platinum General Insurance. Jim left the insurance company approximately ten years later and joined the IT team at the worlds largest volume Ford dealer as IT Director.

Meagen Brooks

Meagen Brooks is a Graphic Designer in ADG Local 800, having worked for television shows such as Weeds, Young Sheldon, Atypical and Saved By The Bell. After having completed her BS in Cinema and Photography at Ithaca College she moved to Los Angeles to pursue her artistic endeavors in both the art and film worlds. She is well versed with Adobe Creative Suite - Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and has working knowledge in Vectorworks and Sketchup Pro. She is also proficient in neon sign design and fabrication. She has been nominated for two Art Director's Guild Awards for graphic design in television and owns and operates her own graphic design company, Flying Dinosaur Designs, LLC, which services multiple industries.

Ervin Natareno

Ervin is a web developer who has 4 years of experience building websites for small to medium sized businesses. Before starting a career in web developement, he spent 5+ years as an Insurance Claims Analyst, working with different web applications, analysts and customers he then noticed they often faced reoccurring issues. His desire to offer solutions and mitigate these issues along with his deep interest in technology led him down the path of web development. He loves to create sites built to look and perform beautifully on all types of devices, with page structures that are logical and help users (and search engines) browse efficiently.
